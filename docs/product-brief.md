# Product Brief

## Product

This app is a mobile-first church leadership operations PWA for company-based reporting, follow-up, task assignment, and internal communication.

The MVP is leadership-only. It is not a public church website, congregation portal, livestreaming platform, finance system, or general church management suite.

## Problem

Church leaders currently send operational updates through scattered WhatsApp messages and informal follow-ups. This makes it hard for admins to see who was absent, what needs attention, who owns each follow-up, and whether leaders have completed assigned tasks.

## Core Workflow

1. A company leader logs in.
2. The leader sees their own company.
3. The leader submits a weekly company report.
4. The leader records absentees.
5. Follow-up cases are created from report and absentee data.
6. Admins review the Monday follow-up list.
7. Admins assign follow-up tasks.
8. Leaders complete assigned tasks.
9. Announcements and read receipts support leadership communication.

## Product Backbone

Companies and weekly reports are the backbone of the product.

Supporting areas include:

- Units
- Follow-up tasks
- Events
- Announcements
- Documents

These features should support the company workflow rather than compete with it.

## MVP Goal

The MVP should prove that church leaders can stop sending scattered WhatsApp reports and instead use one structured workflow for weekly company reporting, absentees, follow-up, assignments, and simple communication.
